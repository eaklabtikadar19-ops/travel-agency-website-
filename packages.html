Offbeat Darjeeling Retreat

Type: Nature · Leisure · Culture Best For: Couples · Families · Photographers
An offbeat Himalayan loop through pine forests, riverside villages, heritage tea estates, and mist-wrapped hilltops — far from the tourist trail.
Route: NJP → Kurseong → Jogighat → Dawaipani → Rishihat → Tabbakoshi → Mirik → NJP
Package Highlights
Kurseong Heritage Circuit Sittong & Mongpoo Exploration Dawaipani Pine Forests Lepchajagat & Mini Pahalgam Gopaldhara Tea Estate Mirik Lake & Bokar Monastery
Day 1 — NJP/Bagdogra to Jogighat (Sittong)
Begin your journey to Darjeeling through the scenic hill town of Kurseong, passing through lush tea gardens, misty forests, and picturesque mountain roads.
  • Kurseong View Point & Giddapahar
  • Dow Hill Forest Area & Chimney Village
  • Hanuman Tak View Point
Meals: Lunch · Snacks · Dinner
Day 2 — Jogighat to Dawaipani
Travel through the scenic hills of Sittong and Mongpoo, exploring serene monasteries, heritage sites, and forest lakes before reaching Dawaipani.
  • Ahaldara View Point & Sittong Monastery
  • Jogighat Bridge & Namthing Pokhri
  • Rabindra Bhavan, Mongpoo
Meals: Breakfast · Lunch · Snacks · Dinner
Day 3 — Dawaipani to Rishihat
Bid farewell to Dawaipani and proceed towards Rishihat — a hidden gem nestled amidst lush tea gardens and rolling Himalayan hills.
Meals: Breakfast · Lunch · Snacks · Dinner
Day 4 — Rishihat to Tabbakoshi
Travel through scenic pine forests, lush tea gardens, and breathtaking Himalayan viewpoints to the peaceful riverside village of Tabbakoshi.
  • Lepchajagat & Majidhura (Mini Pahalgam)
  • Simana View Point
  • Gopaldhara Tea Garden
Meals: Breakfast · Lunch · Snacks · Dinner
Day 5 — Tabbakoshi to NJP
Bid farewell to peaceful Tabbakoshi and proceed towards NJP through tea gardens and charming Himalayan landscapes.
  • Mirik Lake & City Explore
  • Bokar Monastery
  • Tingling View Point
Meals: Breakfast
